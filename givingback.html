MoJazz Dollies is a community summer outreach day event that targets girls ages 5-12 years old. These girls are chosen from shelters, boys & girls clubs, or programs that cater to disadvantage girls. The object of the program is to teach girls the importance of self-love.

  • Introduce them to the art of beautifying themselves
  • Embrace who she is
  • Self Disciplineâ€“ how to get along with others
  • Enhance communication skills
  • An outlet for girls to enjoy being a girl

Photographs are taken of the girls so they can remember how beautiful and strong they are. Brunch, lunch & snacks are served to the girls. MoJazz Dollies goals are to make the girls feel empowered, beautiful and limitless in life.

MoJazz Little Dollies is a day full of fun for little girls. The girls enjoy a day of hair and make-up little girl style.
